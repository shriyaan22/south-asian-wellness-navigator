The Seven Dimensions of the Whole Self

Our well-being is holistic and interconnected. Explore resources dedicated to each dimension of health, grounded in cultural and community context, by clicking on a card below.

1. Body

Physical health, somatic connection, traditional practices (Ayurveda, Yoga, etc.).

2. Mind

Emotional, cognitive, and professional mental health support, anti-stigma resources.

3. Soul

Spiritual grounding, existential purpose, faith-based guidance, meditation, and meaning-making.

4. Family

Intergenerational dynamics, conflict mediation, kinship support, and family acceptance.

5. Community

Social networks, civic engagement, 'seva' (selfless service), and reducing social isolation.

6. Work/Study

Academic pressure, career burnout, professional networking, and financial stress resources.

7. Identity & Culture

Diaspora identity, caste equity, LGBTQ+ support, and cultural affirmation/belonging.

This navigator recognizes that well-being is not just the absence of illness, but a balanced state across all areas of life, deeply influenced by cultural context.
